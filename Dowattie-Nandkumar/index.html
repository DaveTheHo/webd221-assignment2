I got three words for you, Treat Yo Self.

Follow the absurd antics of an Indiana town's public officials as they pursue sundry projects to make their city a better place. Filled with humour and loveable characters; Parks and Rec has completed 6 seasons with a 7th season on the way. NBC's Parks and Recreation has an all star cast: Amy Poehler, Rob Lowe, Nick Offerman, Rashida Jones, and Chris Pratt.
